FAQ

  • Where can I buy the EarthScope?
    The Earthscope will be sold in your local electronics and gardening stores.
  • Is it easy to setup?
    Each Earthscope comes with an easy 3 step setup guide. The approximated setup time is 15 minutes.
  • How do I connect to the device via smartphone?
    The Earthscope will connect to smart phones via bluetooth.
  • Can I control the ideal moisture levels depending on various plants’ needs?
    Our Earthscope app will come complete with the ability to customize the moisture range ideal for your plant.
  • How will I know when plants need moisture?
    With user generated warnings, the Earthscope can warn you when your plant becomes too dry or too moist.
  • Can I setup a schedule to adjust how frequently my plants are monitored?
    The Earthscope allows users to schedule and regulate how often their plants' moisture levels are documented.
